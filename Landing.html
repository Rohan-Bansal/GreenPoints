Green Points is a website that allows you to help the environment one task at a time.
Your tasks will help you create sustainable habits.
Each day, you will receive notifications, prompting you to complete your tasks.
Everytime you complete a task, you get a green point. The more green points you get, the higher your score will be on the leaderboard.

Creating a sustainable society, one task at a time.
